Nomenclature Type is an independent typography project created with the goal of proliferating quality, affordable typefaces. All typefaces are designed and developed by Alex Sweet and are currently free to download for both personal and commercial use. We keep a close eye on typographic trends, balancing the need for avant garde typography with classic principles in an effort to produce efficient, unique fonts that won't lose their edge.

Currently all retail typefaces are available open source and free to download. Many are still in beta; updates are published regularly and improvements are continually being made. If you see something you like that needs customizing to your specs feel free to reach out. For inquiries about custom typefaces, logo design, typemarks, and all things type you can reach me at hello@alexsweet.design
